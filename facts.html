Fun Facts

  • There are 440 known shark species of sharks.
  • The largest species of sharks are the whale sharks which can grow up to 60 feet long.
  • The smallest species of sharks are the dwarf lantern sharks and it can be as short as 4 inches.
  • Sharks can detect the scent of blood from miles away. This is countered by their very bad eyesight.
  • Scientist believe that sharks most likely attack humans because they mistake humans for fishes.
  • Shark have no bones.Their skeletons are made up of cartilage and tissues just like jelly fish and rays.
  • Female sharks have thicker skin than male sharks because male sharks bite during mating.
