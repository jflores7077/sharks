Species:

Hammerheads

one of the most weirdest looking creatures that live in the sea.They live deep in the ocean near the sea floor. Their hammer shaped heads is actually used to pin their prey (mostly stingrays) onto the sea floor.

Whale Shark

biggest living species of sharks today. Ironically they are also one of the mort passive species. They mostly feed on plankton and crayfish by using its gills as suction filters.

SawSharks

long saw like mouth that is used to dismantle its prey. It is one of the smaller shark species but very aggressive.

Basking Shark

second largest species of shark. They have extremely huge mouths that they use as a basket to pick up small fish. They have huge gills that help them filter out all the water from the fish.

Basking Shark

very fast sharks. Just by looking at them should tell you why. Their tails are extremely long making it easy to slash through water and catch up to their next victim. They use their tail as a whip to stun their prey and then finish them of with their teeth.
